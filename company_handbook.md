# Company Handbook

## Mission
TechCorp builds AI-powered tools that help teams work smarter.

## Our Values
- **Innovation**: We ship fast and iterate based on feedback.
- **Transparency**: Open communication at every level.
- **Customer First**: Every decision starts with user impact.

## Departments

### Engineering
Responsible for building and maintaining all software products.
Team size: 45 engineers across backend, frontend, and ML.

### Data & Analytics
Owns the data pipeline, dashboards, and ML model training.
Team size: 12 analysts and 6 data scientists.

### Product
Defines roadmap, runs discovery, and coordinates releases.
Team size: 8 product managers.

## Leave Policy
- Annual Leave: 20 days per year
- Sick Leave: 10 days per year
- Parental Leave: 16 weeks fully paid

## Remote Work
TechCorp is hybrid-first. Employees may work remotely up to 4 days per week.
All-hands meetings are held every Tuesday at 10:00 AM.
